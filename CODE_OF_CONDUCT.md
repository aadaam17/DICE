# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in DICE respectful, focused, and welcoming.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction.
- Keep technical disagreements focused on the work.
- Avoid harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or threats.
- Personal attacks.
- Publishing private information.
- Repeated disruption of project discussion.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when
behavior harms the project or community.
